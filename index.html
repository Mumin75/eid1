Eid Zakat, also known as Zakat al-Fitr or Fitrana, is a mandatory charitable donation
given by Muslims at the end of Ramadan, before the Eid al-Fitr prayer. It's a
purification for the fasting person and ensures that the less
fortunate can celebrate Eid with dignity.
